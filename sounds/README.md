# Sound Files for Space Shooter Game

This folder contains all the sound effects and music tracks for the space shooter game.

## Sound Files List:

### Music Tracks:
- `menu-music.mp3` - Background music for menus
- `game-music.mp3` - Background music during gameplay

### Sound Effects:
- `player-shoot.wav` - Player firing sound
- `enemy-shoot.wav` - Enemy firing sound
- `enemy-destroy.wav` - Enemy destruction sound
- `boss-destroy.wav` - Boss destruction sound
- `meteor-destroy.wav` - Meteor destruction sound
- `player-hit.wav` - Player taking damage sound
- `powerup.wav` - Power-up collection sound
- `bullet-collision.wav` - Bullet collision sound
- `boss-appear.wav` - Boss appearance sound
- `level-complete.wav` - Level completion sound
- `game-over.wav` - Game over sound

## How to Download Sounds:

You can download free sound effects from these sources:

### 1. OpenGameArt.org
- Visit: https://opengameart.org/
- Search for "space shooter sounds"
- Download royalty-free sounds

### 2. Freesound.org
- Visit: https://freesound.org/
- Create free account
- Search for specific sounds
- Download in WAV/MP3 format

### 3. Zapsplat.com
- Visit: https://zapsplat.com/
- Free account required
- High-quality sound effects

### 4. Quick Sound Generation (Recommended):

For instant setup, you can use online sound generators:

#### sfxr.me or jfxr.frozenfractal.com
- Generate 8-bit style sound effects
- Export as WAV files
- Perfect for retro space shooter sounds

### Suggested Sound Types:

1. **player-shoot.wav**: Short "pew" or "zap" sound
2. **enemy-shoot.wav**: Deeper "thoom" or "blast" sound
3. **enemy-destroy.wav**: Explosion or "boom" sound
4. **boss-destroy.wav**: Large explosion sound
5. **meteor-destroy.wav**: Rock breaking/explosion sound
6. **player-hit.wav**: Damage/hurt sound
7. **powerup.wav**: Positive chime or "ding" sound
8. **bullet-collision.wav**: Small impact sound
9. **boss-appear.wav**: Dramatic entrance sound
10. **level-complete.wav**: Victory fanfare
11. **game-over.wav**: Sad/dramatic ending sound
12. **menu-music.mp3**: Ambient space music (looping)
13. **game-music.mp3**: Action-packed battle music (looping)

## File Format Requirements:
- Sound effects: WAV or MP3 format
- Music: MP3 format recommended
- Keep file sizes reasonable (under 1MB per sound effect)
- Music tracks can be 2-5MB

## Installation:
1. Download your chosen sound files
2. Rename them to match the filenames above
3. Place them in this /sounds/ folder
4. The game will automatically load them!

## Note:
The game includes fallback silence if sound files are missing, so it will work even without sounds installed.
